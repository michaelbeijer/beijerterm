# meeleveren

**See also**: bijleveren (bijzenden)

1. # Dutch
- meeleveren
1. # English
- delivery with …, deliver together with …, deliver along with … 
- supply with …, supply together with …, supply along with …   (Indien gewenst kunnen wij certificaten meeleveren bij onze producten. = We can also **supply** certificates **with** our products if required.)
- include (Er wordt geen extra batterij meegeleverd. = No extra battery is **included**.)
- also send, send along (Indien files worden aangeleverd die gecomprimeerd zijn, programma meeleveren om te decomprimeren. = If compressed files are supplied, please **also send** the program to decompress them.)
