# datalogist

Essentially, a **datalogist** is someone involved with the study, analysis, and processing of data (or ‘**datalogy**’), whether through specific tools and techniques or through a broader understanding of the field of datalogy.
 
 Etymology of ‘datalogy’:
 From data +‎ -logy, from Danish datalogi. Proposed for use in English by Danish scientist Peter Naur. 
 
 Synonyms of ‘datalogy’:
 • computer science
 • computing
 
 Synonym of ‘datalogist’: 
 • computer scientist
