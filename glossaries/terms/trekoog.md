# trekoog

1. # Dutch
- trekoog

1. # English
- (for heavy-duty towing hardware) drawbar eye; drawing eye; towing eye; draw eye (a forged ring for hitches, trailers, vehicles, etc.)
- (aka lashing eye/tie-down eye, for function-first wording) eyelet (Note: If a figure shows no grommet or metal insert, **eye** or **lashing hole** is also acceptable; otherwise, stick with **eyelet**.)
