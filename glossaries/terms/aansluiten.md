# aansluiten

**See also**: aansluiten bij

1. # Dutch
- aansluiten
1. # English
- [CHEM.TECHN.] (of e.g. atoms) bonded
- (e.g. clothes) close-fitting, tight-fitting
- (verbonden) connected, joined, linked 
- (gesloten zonder tussenruimte) closed, linked up, (voegen) butted, abutted
- abutting (of a building or an area of land) be next to or have a common boundary with: "gardens abutting Great Prescott Street") (touch or lean on: "masonry may crumble where a roof abuts it")
- affiliated
- attached
- plugged
- plugged in
- joined
- enrolled
- connected
- plugged 
- plugged in
- tied
