# bindgaren

"Binding yarn" is commonly used in textile engineering to describe yarns that hold multiple fabric layers together.
1. # Dutch
- bindgaren (‘stof­naam; boek­bin­de­rij sterk dun touw om mee te bin­den’, Van Dale)

1. # English
- [TEX.] coarse thread, string (Comprehensive Dictionary of Industry and Technology (Graham P. Oxtoby)) 
- twine (Standaard Bedrijfsindeling codes (SBI-codes))
- binding yarn (Clothing industry glossary (AMTS Technique de Confection)(NL-FR-EN-DE)(Part 1: A to F))

1. # French

- fil à brocher (**IATE**: https://iate.europa.eu/search/result/1739399159192/1)
