# hoogtestage vs hoogtetraining

- **hoogtestage** = a "high altitude training camp" (HATC) or "training camp held at high altitude" (e.g., in the mountains) 
- **hoogtetraining** "(high) altitude training" (in general; relates to a type of training that takes advantage of reduced oxygen availability, which can occur either naturally or artificially)
