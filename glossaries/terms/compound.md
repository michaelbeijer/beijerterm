1. # Wikipedia: ‘chemical compound’
 **samengestelde stof / chemische verbinding** 
 = ‘een stof die wordt gevormd door de chemische binding tussen de atomen van twee of meer chemische elementen’ (https://nl.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samengestelde_stof)
 
 **chemical compound** 
 = ‘a chemical substance composed of many identical molecules (or molecular entities) composed of atoms from more than one element held together by chemical bonds. A molecule consisting of atoms of only one element is therefore not a compound’ (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chemical_compound)
