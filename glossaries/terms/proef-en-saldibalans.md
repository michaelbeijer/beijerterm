# proef- en saldibalans

1. # Dutch
- proef- en saldibalans

1. # English
- trial balance (proef- en saldibalans = trial balance; ‘A report that lists the balances of ledger accounts for a specified time period.’; src: Microsoft Terminology Collection (33,004 Dutch-English entries)(Part 3))
- trial balance sheet (jarry (X) (https://www.proz.com/kudoz/dutch-to-english/finance-general/2484490-saldibalans.html))
- trial balances showing the totals and balances (of all accounts in the ledger) (https://www.proz.com/kudoz/dutch-to-english/accounting/7204460-proef-en-saldibalansen.html) (Kitty Brussaard)
- trial balance(s) and any summary trial balance(s) (https://www.proz.com/kudoz/dutch-to-english/accounting/7204460-proef-en-saldibalansen.html) (Steve Robbie)
- unadjusted and adjusted trial balance(s) (https://www.proz.com/kudoz/dutch-to-english/accounting/7204460-proef-en-saldibalansen.html) (Steve Robbie, "in the event that Sam Habach is right")
- trial and closing balance(s) (https://www.proz.com/kudoz/dutch-to-english/accounting/7204460-proef-en-saldibalansen.html) (Sam Habach, "because proefbalans refers to the preliminary trial balance, and saldibalans often refers to the finalized or adjusted balances")
