# föhnborstel

- *Föhnborstel** is commonly translated as one of the following, depending on the market and exact design:

1. **Hot air brush**
1. **Airstyler**
1. **Hair dryer brush**
1. **Styling brush with hot air**
1. **Hot air styling brush**
1. **Blow-dry brush** (UK English, sometimes used for paddle-style brushes but can be ambiguous)

1. ## Recommendations

- **Hot air brush** is the most widely used and recognised term in English for this kind of device, especially in manuals and retail.
- **Airstyler** is often used as a brand or product name (e.g., Philips Airstyler) and is more common in the UK and EU, but is less generic.
- **Hair dryer brush** is used, but slightly less precise than hot air brush.

1. ## Official Equivalents (for reference)

- **Philips** and **Babyliss** call similar products "hot air brush" or "airstyler" in their English documentation.
