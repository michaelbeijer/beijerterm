# dambreedte

When translating "dambreedte" (in Dutch) in the context of gaskets, the following terms can be used interchangeably, depending on the precise context:
 
 • **wall width**
 • **gasket width**
 • **seal width** (with the understanding that it may specifically refer to the compressed portion forming the seal)
