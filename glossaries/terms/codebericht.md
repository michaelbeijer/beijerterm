# codebericht

See also: open taal
1. # codebericht
- **codebericht** – diplomatic/telegraphic jargon for a coded or encrypted telegram. Opposite of *open taal* (plain text / clear text). Equivalent English terms include: **coded message**, **coded telegram**, **encrypted message**, **cipher message**, or **in code**.  

1. ## Dutch synonyms / variants
- **codebericht**
- **gecodeerd bericht**
- **gecodeerde telegram**
- **versleuteld bericht**

1. ## English synonyms / equivalents
- **coded message**  
- **coded telegram**  
- **cipher message**  
- **encrypted message**  
- **encoded message**  
- **in code**  

1. ## Notes
Frequently used in Foreign Ministry instructions, e.g. *“Op een codeber. mag telegr. niet in open taal worden geantwoord …”* (A coded message may not be answered in clear text …). Indicates that sensitive diplomatic telegrams must be transmitted in code or cipher, not in plain language.
