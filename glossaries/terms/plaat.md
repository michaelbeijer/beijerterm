1. ## Oxforddictionaries.com
 **plate**:
 A thin, flat sheet or strip of metal or other material, typically one used to join or strengthen things or forming part of a machine
 
 **board**:
 A long, thin, flat piece of wood or other hard material, used for floors or other building purposes
 
 **sheet**:
 A broad flat piece of material such as metal or glass
 
 **panel**:
 A flat or curved component, typically rectangular, that forms or is set into the surface of a door, wall, or ceiling
 
 **slab**:
 A large, thick, flat piece of stone or concrete, typically square or rectangular in shape
