# sociale inspectie

1. # Dutch
- **sociale inspectie**

1. # English
- *labor inspection service (Belgium)*
- *Belgian social inspection*

1. # Usage examples
- Uiterlijk op 15 december moet een kopie van het bericht met de vervangingsdagen worden bezorgd aan de **sociale inspectie**.
- By 15 December at the latest, a copy of the notice with the replacement days must be sent to the Belgian *labor inspection service* (**sociale inspectie**).

1. # Explanation
In Belgium, the **sociale inspectie** refers to the federal labor inspection services that supervise compliance with labor law and social security legislation. These services check whether employers respect rules on working time, holidays, wages, health and safety, and social security contributions. Inspectors can enter workplaces, question employers and employees, examine documents, and impose penalties in case of infringements.  

The **sociale inspectie** is part of the broader system of *sociale zekerheid* (social security) enforcement in Belgium and works alongside other bodies such as the *Rijksdienst voor Sociale Zekerheid* (RSZ/ONSS).
