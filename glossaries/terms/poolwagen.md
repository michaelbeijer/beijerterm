# poolwagen

Note 1: Sometimes, "poolwagen" (pool vehicle) is used to mean "bedrijfsvoertuig"/"bedrijfswagen" (i.e., company car)! A company car is allocated to an individual employee, whereas a pool vehicle or pool car is used by multiple employees and parked at work when not in use.

 Note 2: See also: aanloopwagen.

1. # Dutch
- poolvoertuig, pool-voertuig, pool voertuig
- poolwagen, pool-wagen, pool wagen
- poolauto, pool-auto, pool auto
- deelauto 

1. # English
- pool vehicle
- pool car
