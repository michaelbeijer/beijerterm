# verladen

1. # Dutch
- verladen

1. # English
1. **(inladen, in la­ding ne­men)** ship (JurLex, Van Dale)
1. **(overladen, in een an­der schip of voer­tuig la­den)** reship, load onto another wagon/ship (JurLex, Van Dale)
1. transshipment, transfer of goods (ook: overslag, overlading, verscheping, IATE (**IATE**: https://iate.europa.eu/search/standard/result/byUrl/1))
1. loading (see Examples below)
