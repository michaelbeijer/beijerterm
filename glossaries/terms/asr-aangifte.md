# ASR-aangifte

In a Belgian payroll and HR context, "ASR" stands for:
 
 "Aangifte Sociaal Risico" (Declaration of Social Risk)
 
 These are formal notifications made by employers to the Belgian social security authorities to report specific social risk events affecting employees, such as:
 
 • Start or end of employment
 • Unemployment
 • Sickness
 • Accident at work
 • Maternity or parental leave
