1. # Co-vergisting van verschillende biomassastromen
 co-vergisting       
 Definition: gelijktijdig vergisten van verschillende biomassastromen in een vergistingsinstallatie, waarbij biogas wordt geproduceerd
 Definition reference: naar: Bio-energie Noord, http://www.bioenergienoord.nl/random/covergisting_v29mei07.pdf (8.7.09)
 =
 co-digestion       
 Definition: process whereby one or more waste types are digested in a mixture, in order to enhance digester efficiency and increase biogas yield
 Definition reference: 'co-digestion'. Anaerobic Digestion Strategy and Action Plan, Department for Environment, Food and Rural Affairs, 2011
