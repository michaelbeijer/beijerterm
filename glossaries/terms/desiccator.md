# desiccator

[thumb|OED](File:exsiccator.png)

1. # English
- desiccator (“a glass container or other apparatus holding a drying agent for removing moisture from specimens and protecting them from water vapour in the air.” (https://premium.oxforddictionaries.com/definition/english/desiccator))
- exsiccator (“an apparatus for drying substances or objects, typically used in a laboratory setting” - collinsdictionary.com)
