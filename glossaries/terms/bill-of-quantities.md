# bill of quantities

1. # English
- Bill of Quantities (BoQ/B.o.Q.)
1. ### Definition
 "bill of quantities: the document containing an itemized breakdown of the tasks to be carried out in a unit price contract, indicating a quantity for each item and the corresponding unit price," (https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/HTML/?uri=CELEX:31992D0097&rid=9)
 
 "Bill of Quantities" is the document in which the Contractor indicates the cost of the Works, on the basis of the foreseen quantities of items of work and the fixed unit prices applicable to them." (https://popp.undp.org/UNDP_POPP_DOCUMENT_LIBRARY/Public/PSU_Award%20and%20Management%20of%20Contract_Model%20Contract%20for%20Civil%20Works.docx )
