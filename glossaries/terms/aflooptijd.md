# aflooptijd

See also: oplooptijd, aanlooptijd
1. # Dutch
- aflooptijd

1. # English
- (van een motor) run-down time (**IATE Definition**: said of an engine when it falls off to zero revolutions per minute after flameout or failure. **Definition reference**: according to Jane's Aerospace Dict., B.Gunston, U.K.,1986 (https://iate.europa.eu/search/result/1735943996889/1))
- expiration time (https://iate.europa.eu/search/result/1735943996889/1)
- **(welding)** ramp-down time, ramp down time (**Welding context: ramp-down time** is the time it takes for the electrical current to gradually decrease to zero after welding is complete. This prevents sudden drops in current, which could lead to cracks or weak points in the weld. See also ‘ramp-up time’ (oplooptijd))
