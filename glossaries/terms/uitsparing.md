# uitsparing

1. # English
; opengelaten ruimte, niet-ingevulde plek:
: – relief
: – structural opening
: – cut-away
: – cutout, cut-out (the hole, usually round or rectangular, cut into a metal panel in order to mount a connector. It may also include holes for mounting screws or bolts)
: – (inkeping) notch
: – (groter) recess
: – indentation
: – slot (de schoot kan in de uitsparing schuiven = engagement of the catch in the slot)

: – saving

; decorative additions to a vehicle, typically the upholstery or interior lining of a car:
: – decorative trim (see IATE link below)
