# saccharine

**saccharine**:
 1. excessively sweet or sentimental: saccharine music
 2. *dated* relating to or containing sugar; sugary.
 
 another term for ‘saccharin’

 **saccharin**:
 1. a sweet-tasting synthetic compound used in food and drink as a substitute for sugar.
