# vraagbaak

1. # Dutch
- vraagbaak

- Van Dale*:

1. persoon tot wie mensen zich richten met moeilijke vragen

2. naslagwerk

1. # English
- (book) manual, handbook 
- (human) guru, expert 
- (general) source of information
- walking encyclopaedia ('Her extensive knowledge on the various subjects and clients at Translavic make her a valued walking encyclopaedia among her colleagues.')
- go-to person (Ik wil hier de vraagbaak zijn van iets. = I want to be the go-to person on something around here.)
- help desk (Onze rol: fabrikant, vraagbaak en kennispunt. = Our role: manufacturer, help-desk and centre of expertise.)
