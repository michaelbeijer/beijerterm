# slib

1. # Dutch
- slib

1. # English
- (slik, schor) mud-flat, mud flat 
- [CIV.ENG.] mud, sludge 
- (neerslag/bezinksel van onzuivere stoffen, rioolspecie) sludge, slurry 
- (slijk, slik, modder) sludge, silt, slime, ooze, warp, sediment, deposit
- [GEOL.] (rivierslib) alluvial deposit, alluvium
- (m.b.t. ertsen) gangue
- (kleverige/natte massa) ooze, mud, mire, slime

1. # Usage examples
- slib van een slijpsteen = (grinding) slurry, swarf, nourings

1. # silt
 **1.** **fine sand, clay, or other material carried by running water and deposited as a sediment, especially in a channel or harbour.** (synonyms: sediment, deposit, alluvium, mud, slime, ooze, sludge; sand, clay)
 **1.1** [noun](count) a bed or layer of silt.
 **1.2** technical: sediment whose particles are between clay and sand in size (typically 0.002–0.06 mm).
