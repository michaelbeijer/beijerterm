# sash

1. # English
- sash 
- * ‘A frame holding the glass in a window, typically one of two sliding frames in a sash window.’ **(Oxforddictionaries.com)**
- * ‘**A frame, usually of wood, rebated and fitted with one or more panes of glass forming a window or part of a window; esp. a sliding frame or each of the two sliding frames of a sash window** n. Also (? now only U.S.) applied to a casement.’ **(OED** (https://www.oed.com/dictionary/sash_n2?tl=true)**)**
