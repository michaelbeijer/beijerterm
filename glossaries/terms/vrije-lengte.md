# vrije lengte

Note:
 • ‘Unbraced length’ is the more formal engineering term, especially in codes/standards (AISC, Eurocode).
 • ‘Unsupported length’ is more descriptive/accessible and often used in patents to aid readability.
1. # Dictionary of Building and Civil Engineering English, German, French, Dutch, Russian by S. N. Korchomkin, S. V. Kurbatov, N. B. Sheikhon, G. B. Vilj.pdf

- **English**: unbraced length
- **German**: freie Lange, Iichte Weite 
- **French**: longueur libre
- **Dutch**: overspanning, vrije lengte
