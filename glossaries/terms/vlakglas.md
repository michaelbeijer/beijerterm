# vlakglas

1. # Dutch
- vlakglas

1. # English
- flat glass; sheet glass
- In industrial contexts, vlakglas refers to flat glass products such as window panes, mirrors, façades, automotive glass, and architectural glazing. It is distinguished from [(hollow glass)](holglas)(holglas), which refers to bottles, jars, and other container glass.

1. # Usage examples
- "Refractaire en afdichtingsmaterialen voor vlak- en holglasproductie met toepassingen in smelten, vormen en koelen."  
→ Refractory and sealing materials for flat and hollow glass production with applications in melting, forming, and cooling. (Example phrase from a Dutch technical brochure on refractory and sealing materials.)

- "De bouwsector is de grootste afnemer van vlakglas, onder meer voor ramen, deuren en gevelbekleding."  
→ The construction sector is the largest consumer of flat glass, particularly for windows, doors, and façade cladding. (International Commission on Glass, 'Flat Glass Applications', https://icglass.org)
