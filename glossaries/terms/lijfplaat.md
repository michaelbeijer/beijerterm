# lijfplaat

1. # Dutch

- lijfplaat

1. # English

- web plate (the vertical plate connecting the upper and lower flanges or cover plates of the girder. (CMAA Specifications for Electric Overhead Traveling Cranes, 70-7 Glossary))
- shear web (gegolfde lijfplaat = corrugated shear web) (**IATE** Definition: a shear-carrying member, stiffened by virtue of being corrugated; Definition reference: Multilingual Aeronautical Dictionary (AGARD, 1980)

https://iate.europa.eu/search/standard/result/byUrl/1)
- web (of a beam) (lijfplaat van een balk (https://juremy.com/search#src=nld&dst=eng&q=lijfplaat))
- body plate
