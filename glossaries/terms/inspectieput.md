# inspectieput

1. # Dutch
- inspectieput

1. # English
- inspection well, observation well (wells in a pipeline to facilitate the inspection of the operation of the drain. ICID) 
- inspection pit (a pit excavated at the junction of a lateral and main drain; acts as a silt trap and is used to facilitate inspection of a composite tile drainage system. ICID) 
- manhole, sewerage manhole, inspection chamber (chamber entered via a hole through which a man may descend or creep into a drain, sewer, steam boiler, parts of machinery, etc., for cleaning or repairing) 
- monitoring well (IATE term note: bodemverontreiniging) 
- drainage channel 
- hatch-box, unblocking point (ontstoppingspunt/inspectieput)[Water Supply and Building Sanitation](L.B.Escritt,)
