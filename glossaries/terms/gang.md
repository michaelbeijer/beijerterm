# gang

<blockquote>Een **dike** (Britse spelling: ***dyke***) of **gang** is in de geologie een plaatvormig lichaam dat oorspronkelijk horizontale gesteentelagen doorsnijdt. Dikes kunnen zowel magmatisch als sedimentair van oorsprong zijn. (https://nl.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dike_(geologie)<nowiki/>)</blockquote><blockquote>In geology, a **dike** or **dyke** is a sheet of rock that is formed in a fracture of a pre-existing rock body. Dikes can be either magmatic or sedimentary in origin. Magmatic dikes form when magma flows into a crack then solidifies as a sheet intrusion, either cutting across layers of rock or through a contiguous mass of rock. Clastic dikes are formed when sediment fills a pre-existing crack. (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dike_(geology)<nowiki/>)</blockquote>
