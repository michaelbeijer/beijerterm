# funderingsadvies

1. # Dutch
- funderingsadvies
- funderingstechnisch advies

1. # English
- foundation assessment (involves inspecting or evaluating existing foundations, e.g., structural integrity, suitability for building, etc.)
- foundation advice (refers more generally to recommendations about how to design or build a foundation, e.g. in a geotechnical report)
- geotechnical advice (broader and often used when soil conditions and foundation design are considered together)
- foundation engineering report (if the advice is formalised into a report, especially in construction contexts)
- subsoil and foundation advice (sometimes used when soil analysis is part of it)
