# opdrachtgever

1. # Dutch
- opdrachtgever

1. # English
- client (**Definition:** any natural or legal person for whom a project is carried out

- *Definition reference:** Council Directive 92/57/EEC on the implementation of minimum safety and health requirements at temporary or mobile constructions sites. CELEX:31992L0057/EN)
- customer
- contracting party
- (juridisch en handel, principaal) principal (https://iate.europa.eu/entry/result/1239370/la-mul-nl-en)
- business owner (contracting party who initiates a project and will own the completed project (https://iate.europa.eu/entry/result/761423/la-mul-nl-en)), project owner, client
- (van kunstenaar) patron
- sponsor (opdrachtgever van een klinische proef)
- owner, building owner, awarding authority (https://iate.europa.eu/entry/result/1581588/la-mul-nl-en)
- originator (**person who gives an instruction authorising a transfer of funds or value to a beneficiary** (https://iate.europa.eu/entry/result/1557064/la-mul-nl-en)), initiator, ordering customer, transferor, instructing party
- [COMP.] (m.b.t. ITIL service management) executive 
- [COMP.] (eigenaar van een project) senior responsible owner

1. # Usage examples
- de gemeente is de opdrachtgever van het standbeeld = the local authority has commissioned the statue
- (figuurlijk) zich achter zijn opdrachtgever verschuilen = take refuge/hide behind one's principal/client
