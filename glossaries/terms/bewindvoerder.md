# bewindvoerder

1. # Dutch
- bewindvoerder
- curator (bij handelingsonbekwaamheid)

1. # English
- (afwezige) administrator
- guardian (*A person who looks after and is legally responsible for someone who is unable to manage their own affairs, especially an incompetent or disabled person or a child whose parents have died.* (https://premium.oxforddictionaries.com/definition/american_english/guardian))
- (EIB, Wereldbank) director
- (erfenis met testament, meerderjarige, minderjarige) executor
- (erfenis zonder testament, meerderjarige, minderjarige) administrator
- (schuldsanering) administrator
- (surseance van betaling) administrator

----

- door de rechter benoemde bewindvoerder = administrator appointed by the court
- gerechtelijk bewindvoerder = court-appointed administrator
- provisionele bewindvoerder = interim administrator
- stille bewindvoerder = undisclosed administrator
- voorlopige bewindvoerder = interim administrator
- beschermingsbewindvoerder = protective guardian, protective conservator (US)
