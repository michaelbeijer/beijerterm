# afbindpost

tying-off station
 tying station
 binding station
 
 In the context of a [baler](oprolpers) (aka baling press or roll baler) an ‘afbindpost’ is a dedicated station or part of the machine where a completed cylindrical bale is tied off with binding twine to keep it from unravelling.
