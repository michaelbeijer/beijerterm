1. # English
- glazing bead (“**A glazing bead is a narrow strip of material used to secure glass panes within a window frame.** It can be made from a variety of materials, including wood, vinyl, aluminum, or PVC. The primary purpose of the glazing bead is to hold the glass securely in place and to provide a finished appearance to the window assembly.” (https://windowhardwaredirect.com/blogs/news/what-is-a-glazing-bead-understanding-its-importance-in-windows?srsltid=AfmBOooTQcaPDolxlbt-sVdZHwE8BH8uQK8JWiEiAphICutV4S1JNZQ0)), glazing beading
