# rek bij spanning vs rek bij breuk

- **rek bij spanning; rekspanning** (elongation at stress; elongation under tension; elongation stress; elongation under stress)

This refers to the strain (percentage elongation) of a material at a specific applied stress BEFORE IT BREAKS.

- **rek bij breuk; treksterkte** (elongation at break; breaking elongation)

This is the total elongation of a material WHEN IT BREAKS. It represents the material's ultimate stretchability before failure.
