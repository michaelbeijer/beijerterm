# harde ondergrond

‘[Ondergrond](ondergrond)’ can be tricky to translate as it can have many meanings, such as:

1. # Dutch
- harde ondergrond

1. # English
- hard standing
- hard subsurface (**subsurface** = The stratum or strata below the earth's surface.)
- hard substrate (**substrate** = An underlying substance or layer.)
- hard surface
- hard ground (**ground** = The solid surface of the earth.)
- hard substratum
- hard subsoil (**subsoil** = The soil lying immediately under the surface soil.)
