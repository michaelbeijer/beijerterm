# accommodatie

1. # Dutch
- accommodatie

1. # English
- **[BUILD.] (een onderkomen)** accommodation, space, room, property
- **[OPT.] (aanpassing; (ook m.b.t. het oog))** accommodation (https://nl.wikipedia.org/wiki/Accommodatie_(oog))
- **(voor­zie­ning/voor­zie­ningen)** amenities, facilities/facility (**facility**: **(1)** a place, amenity, or piece of equipment provided for a particular purpose: *cooking facilities, a manufacturing facility* **(1.1)** a special feature of a service or machine, which offers the opportunity to do or benefit from something: *an overdraft facility* **(2)** a natural ability to do or learn something well and easily: *he had a facility for languages* **(2.1)** absence of difficulty or effort: *the pianist played with great facility* **(Oxford Dictionaries Premium)**)
- **(testing iron)** accommodation (IATE Definition: it may occur that a test piece stressed beyond its elastic limit and not recovering after unloading its initial length, on being subjected to a series of loads and unloadings stabilizes at a length slightly different from the initial one. This occurrence is referred to as "accommodation" and is observed with cast iron. Definition reference: ATF dic. idéologique de fonderie)

1. # Usage examples
- er is accommodatie voor tien passagiers = there is facility (UK) / accommodation (US) / are facilities (UK) for ten passengers, ten passengers can be accommodated
- (accommodatie → voor­zie­ning/voor­zie­ningen → mid­del) ge­le­gen­heid om pas­sa­giers te ver­voe­ren = pas­sa­giers­ac­com­mo­datie
- toeristische voorzieningen, toeristische accommodatie = tourist amenities, tourist facilities
