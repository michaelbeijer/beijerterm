# magazijn

[Dale NL-EN: magazijn](thumb|Van)(File:magazijn.png)
1. # Dutch
- magazijn

1. # English
- **(grote winkel)** shop, (Amerikaans-Engels) store; business 
- **(kaartenbak)** card hopper 
- **(van wapens)** arms store, arms depot, armoury 
- **((groot) bergplaats voor waren)** warehouse, supplies depot, store depot, store room, supply room, storehouse, depot, repository, stockroom, storage shed, store shed 
- cartridge, magazine 
- magazine (“a hopper for holding coded magnetic or punched cards ready for input to some device”, **src**: I11.dict.electr., [IATE](https://iate.europa.eu/search/result/1735943996889/1)) 
- magazine (“container for holding workpieces correctly positioned for feeding to a processing station”, **src**: CIRP Dic Production Engineering 3:Sheet Metal Forming, IATE) 
- magazine, magazine feeding attachment, magazine feed, magazine feed attachment, automatic magazine loader, magazine loader (“a feeding attachment for the storage and automatic feed of workpieces; it lacks a mechanism for sorting or arranging them”, **src**: Wüster:Machine Tool 1968, [IATE](https://iate.europa.eu/search/result/1735943996889/1)) 
- **[MACH.] (voor gereedschappen bij CNC-machines)** magazine
- **(van diaprojector)** (slide) tray

1. # Usage examples
- iets uit het magazijn halen = get something from the warehouse/supply room/stockroom
- boeken in het centraal magazijn onderbrengen = store books in the central repository
- centraal magazijn/centrale voorraad = central stock (https://iate.europa.eu/search/result/1735943996889/1)
