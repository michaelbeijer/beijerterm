# aangrijppunt

1. # Dutch
- aangrijppunt

1. # English
- pivot (The central point, pin, or shaft on which a mechanism turns or oscillates. (Oxforddictionaries.com))
- point of activity (mechanical engineering; Punt waar de aanwezige kracht aangrijpt)
- point of engagement
- point of coupling, coupling point
- centre of articulation
- origin of force
