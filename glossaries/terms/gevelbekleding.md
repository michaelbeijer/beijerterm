# gevelbekleding

gevel =
 1. voormuur
 • (house)front
 • façade
 
 2. buitenmuur
 • outside/outer wall

1. # Dutch
- **gevelbekleding** (‘ma­te­ri­aal waar­mee de ge­vel be­kleed is’, Van Dale)

1. # English
- **wall cladding, siding** (OGL, my preferred option)
- facade cladding, sheathing (GWIT)
- facade coping, elevation coping, front coping (https://iate.europa.eu/search/result/1658573455809/1)
