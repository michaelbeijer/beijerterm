# landingsbaan

- *Belgian Dutch**: landingsbaan/landingsbanen (ook wel "tijdskrediet eindeloopbaan" genoemd)

- *English**: "landing jobs" (end-of-career time credit schemes; a Belgian scheme allowing older workers to reduce working hours before retirement)
