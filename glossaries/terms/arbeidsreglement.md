# arbeidsreglement

1. # Dutch
- arbeidsreglement

1. # English
- work rules  
- workplace regulations  
- company work regulations  
- (mandatory) company work regulations (a written set of rules on working hours, rest periods, pay arrangements, etc., that every Belgian employer must draw up and file)

1. # Usage examples
- Het arbeidsreglement bevat onder meer de begin- en einduren van de arbeidstijd, de rustpauzes, de wijze van loonbetaling, de tuchtstraffen en de maatregelen inzake welzijn op het werk. (Federale Overheidsdienst Werkgelegenheid, Arbeid en Sociaal Overleg. [Arbeidsreglement](https://werk.belgie.be/nl/themas/arbeidsreglement). Geraadpleegd op 16 augustus 2025.)  
- The workplace regulations include, among other things, the start and end times of working hours, breaks, the method of wage payment, disciplinary sanctions, and measures relating to wellbeing at work. (Federal Public Service Employment, Labour and Social Dialogue. [Work rules](https://werk.belgie.be/en/themes/work-rules). Accessed 16 August 2025.)
