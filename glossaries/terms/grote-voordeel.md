# grote voordeel

1. # Dutch
- grote voordeel

1. # English
- major advantage
- great advantage
- big advantage
- distinct advantage 
- valuable advantage
- considerable advantage
- large benefit
- huge benefit
- main advantage (note the slightly different meaning here; i.e. it may refer to one particular main advantage, among others)
