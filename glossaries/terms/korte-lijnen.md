# korte lijnen

- Korte lijnen* (korte communicatielijnen, korte lijnen van communicatie, etc.) is one of those Dutch business clichés that doesn’t land well in English if translated literally as *short lines (of communication)*. For marketing and web texts, here are some smoother, idiomatic options you could use depending on tone and emphasis:

1. ## Neutral / businesslike

- **direct communication**
- **efficient communication**
- **clear communication**

1. ## Customer-focused

- **fast feedback**
- **quick response times**
- **direct contact**
- **easy to reach**

1. ## Marketing punch

- **no red tape**
- **straightforward communication**
- **fast, direct contact**
- **streamlined communication**
