# informatiewaarde

- information value ([GWIT](Comprehensive Dictionary of Industry and Technology (Graham P. Oxtoby)), COMP.) 
- measure of information
- * IATE Definition: a function of the frequency of occurrence of a specified event from a set of possible events, conventionally taken as a measure of the relative value of intelligence conveyed by this occurrence

On the one hand, as regards the “**informational content**” of the commodity, it refers directly to the changes taking place in workers’ labor processes ... where the skills involved in direct labor are increasingly skills involving cybernetics and computer control (and horizontal and vertical communication).
