1. # Dutch
- eindvorm

1. # English
- final form (GWIT)
- finished forging (**IATE Definition:** there is no clear English equivalent."Finished Forging" is sometimes used but it is ambiguous as this may also mean a forging after finished machining. **Definition reference:** CIRP Dic Production Engineering 1:Forging and Drop Forging (https://iate.europa.eu/search/result/1736701733517/1))
- (geneesmiddelen in hun eindvorm) finished medicinal products (http://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/nl-en/TXT/?uri=CELEX:62013TJ0472)
