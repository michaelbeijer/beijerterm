# first-tier supplier vs second-tier supplier

See also: toeleverancier
;first-tier supplier
:a company that provides parts and materials directly to a manufacturer of goods: “Assemblers tend to rely on a few first-tier suppliers, and require these suppliers to play a greater role in product design and innovation.”

;second-tier supplier
:a company that supplies materials or parts to another company that then supplies them to a manufacturer: “There is a possibility that the second-tier supplier may supply directly to the wine producer.”
