1. # Usage examples
- Hydrophilic **waterstops** are designed to prevent water from leaking from or into concrete structures by swelling when in contact with water and creating an impenetrable barrier. They can be used for multiple purposes, including protecting structures from the wear and tear of a constant flow of water and maintaining the desired structural integrity.
