# digitalisering

See also: digitise vs digitalise

1. # Dutch
- digitalisering
- digitaliseert
- digitaliseren
- digitalisatie
- informatisering (sometimes also used)

1. # English
- digitisation (scanning, etc.)
- digitalisation (entire company/processes, etc.)
- digitalise, digitalize

1. # Explanation
‘If I scan a document, I digitize it. But I would digitalize a factory.’
