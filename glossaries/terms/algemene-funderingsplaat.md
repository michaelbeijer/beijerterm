# algemene funderingsplaat

1. # Dutch
- algemene funderingsplaat
- zwevende vloerplaat
- plaatfundering

1. # English

- raft foundation
- mat foundation (a reinforced concrete slab that covers the entire footprint of a building, spreading the load over a large area and ‘floating’ on the ground, particularly useful in areas with weak soil.)
- raft footings<br />
