# dingleberry

**1.** a foolish or inept person.
 
 Synonyms: idiot, halfwit, nincompoop, blockhead, buffoon, dunce, dolt, ignoramus, cretin, imbecile, dullard, moron, simpleton
 
 **2.**  vulgar slang
 a particle of faecal matter attached to the anal hair of an animal.
 
 **Origin**
 1920s (originally denoting the southern mountain cranberry): of uncertain origin; perhaps an alteration of dangleberryfrom dangle + berry.
