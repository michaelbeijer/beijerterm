# Elongate

elongate:
 
 **VERB [OBJECT](WITH)**
 * 1. Make (something) longer, especially unusually so in relation to its width: ‘elongate your spine’
 * 1.1 [OBJECT](NO) *mainly Biology* Become longer: ‘the pup's globular body slowly elongates’
 **ADJECTIVE**
 * Long in relation to width; elongated: ‘elongate fishes’
