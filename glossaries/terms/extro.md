# extro

The term "extro" and "extro (gaan)" are non-standard in Dutch. It is sometimes used to mean: disappearing/being removed from the product range, being discontinued or phased out, etc.

1. # Dutch
- extro (gaan)
1. # English
- being discontinued
- being phased out
