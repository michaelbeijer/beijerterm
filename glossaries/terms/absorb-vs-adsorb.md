# absorb vs adsorb

1. ## English

A**b**sorb - This term refers to the process where **one substance is taken into another through permeation or dissolution**. In absorption, the substance fully penetrates into the absorbing material. For example, a sponge absorbs water.</big>

A**d**sorb - This term is used to describe **a process where atoms, ions, or molecules from a substance** (such as a gas or liquid) **adhere to the surface of an adsorbent material**. Adsorption is a surface-based process.</big>
