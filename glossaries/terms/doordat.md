# doordat

1. # Dutch
- doordat

1. # English
- through (the fact that)
- because (of the fact that) 
- owing to (the fact that)
- as a result of (the fact that)
- on account of (the fact that)
- in that (for the reason that)
- for the reason that
- as
- since
