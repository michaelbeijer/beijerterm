# plano

English:
 plano-
 = level; flat:

1. # Dutch
- plano

1. # English
- (plano vouwdoos/doos) carton blank, cardboard blank, blank, flat blank (“The creasing bars or lines of the tool create linear depressions on one side (on the **flat blank**) and a bead-like elevation in the form of a permanent deformation on the opposite side, which is created by the channel-shaped counter-creasing device. The creasing thus also defines the position of the fold line on a folding carton or on a folding box.” (https://www.labelprint24.com/en/magazine/technical-terms-explained-what-does-creasing-mean-761))
- broadsheet (Antiquarian Dictionary in four languages (Boekenvondst.nl)) (a publication consisting usually of a single sheet bearing matter printed on both sides of the sheet (Un.Term.Doc.) (https://iate.europa.eu/search/result/1641854834109/1)

1. # Usage examples
- plano papier (Antalis.nl: glossary of paper industry terms (Dutch, English, French and German)) = flat paper, board; paper, board in the flat (ongevouwen en onopgerolde vellen papier of karton)
- plano doos = cut, blank (https://iate.europa.eu/search/result/1641854834109/1)  
- plano doos en deksel = 2-piece flats with separate slid and bottom (for corner gluing) (https://iate.europa.eu/search/result/1641854834109/1)  
- plano vouwdoos (met plaksluiting) = carton blank (with full plain ends and short side flaps)
- plano vouwdoos voor machinaal opzetten = blank for mechanical erecting/ cartoning machines
- plano vouwdoos (met verschillende sluitingen) = carton blank (with tuck top and full plain end)
- vouwdoos in plano-vorm, niet-opgezette doos = box blank, carton blank
