# stiksteek

1. # Dutch
- stiksteek (stiksteken)
- achtersteek

1. # English
- (machinaal) lock stitch, lockstitch (Clothing industry glossary (AMTS Technique de Confection)(NL-FR-EN-DE)(Part 4: R to Z)) (Oxford Dictionaries Premium: **lock stitch** = “a stitch made by a sewing machine by firmly linking together two threads or stitches. ”)
- (met de hand) back stitch, backstitch (497 Dutch-English sewing terms) (Verklarende woordenlijst naaitermen Engels-Nederlands (naaipatronen.nl)) (Oxford Dictionaries Premium: **backstitch** = “a method of sewing with overlapping stitches”)
- (machinaal) straight stitch (Oxford Dictionaries Premium: **straight stitch** = “a single short separate embroidery stitch.”) (Van Dale NL-EN online)

1. # Usage examples
- stiksteken met een(enkele)draad = lock-stitching with one thread only
