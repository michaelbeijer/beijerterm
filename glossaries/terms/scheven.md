# scheven

- *Dutch**: scheven       

- Definition: grove bestanddelen van vezelachtig materiaal, in halfstof of papier aanwezig ten gevolge van onvoldoende ontsluiting
- Definition reference: NEN 3376

- *English**: shives       

- Term reference: TNC92; ISO 4046:1978
- Definition: fibre bundles present in pulp or paper which derive from incomplete fibre separation during the pulping process
- Definition reference: TNC92
