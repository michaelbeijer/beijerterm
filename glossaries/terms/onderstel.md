# onderstel

1. # Dutch
- onderstel
1. # English
- (**deel van een voorwerp**) support 
- base frame, base
- (‘**a structure made of wood or metal on which the various parts of a machine are assembled**’) (**IATE**: Wüster:Machine Tool 1968) machine frame, frame 
- (‘**base frame of a motor vehicle or other wheeled conveyance**’) (**Oxforddictionaries.com**) chassis/frame (of a vehicle) 
- (**landingsgestel van vliegtuig**) undercarriage, landing gear
- (**van trein, tram**) bogie
- (**of a railway carriage, or other wheeled device**) (**IATE**: https://iate.europa.eu/search/standard/result/1617979657710/1 ) underframe, chassis
- (**van kanon / rijtuig / auto / vliegtuig**) undercarriage
- (**legs; humorous**) pegs
- (**wielstel, loopstel**) (**IATE**: https://iate.europa.eu/search/standard/result/1617979657710/1) mounted axle, mounted wheels on axle, wheels on axle
