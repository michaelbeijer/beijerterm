1. # References
 **pamphlet**: A small booklet or leaflet containing information or arguments about a single subject:
 ‘*It is simply an information pamphlet and a letter describing the company's intent.*’
 <small>synonyms: brochure, leaflet, booklet, circular, flyer, handbill, handout, bill, notice, tract;</small>
 
 **leaflet**: A printed sheet of paper containing information or advertising and usually distributed free: 
 ‘*pick up a leaflet from your local branch*’
 <small>synonyms: pamphlet, booklet, brochure, handbill, circular, flyer, handout, advertisement, bulletin, mailshot, bill, notice;</small>
 
 **brochure**: A small book or magazine containing pictures and information about a product or service:
 ‘*a holiday brochure*’
 <small>synonyms: pamphlet, booklet, prospectus, catalogue, leaflet, handbill, handout, bill, circular, flyer, notice, advertisement;</small>
 
 (Oxforddictionaries.com)
