# opstand

1. # Dutch

- opstand

1. # English
1. raised edge
1. (dakrand) upstand, upturn, water check
1. (geboomte) standing timber
1. (verticale projectie) (vertical) elevation
1. (van winkel, enz.) fittings and fixtures

1. ## Misc.

- jonge opstand = young standing timber
- plattegrond en opstand = plan and elevation
- opstandstrook = upstand strip
