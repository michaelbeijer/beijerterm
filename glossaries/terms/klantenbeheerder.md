1. # Difference between ‘klantenbeheerder’ and ‘relatiebeheerder’

1. ## Analogy:
Think of the **klantenbeheerder** as your **account technician**, and the **relatiebeheerder** as your **account strategist**.

They both deal with the same client, but at different levels of engagement:

- One ensures the machine runs,
- The other ensures the machine is the right one for the job and keeps working for the client long-term.
