# kostprijs

**Definition**: ‘the price, excluding VAT, at which the goods were acquired by the taxable person or, if he made them himself, the cost of their manufacture, including overheads but excluding profit’ (https://iate.europa.eu/search/standard/result/1617921651988/1)
1. # Dutch
- kostprijs, kost prijs, kost-prijs
1. # English
- (accounting) cost, cost price 
- (IFRS) cost
- acquisition price
- prime price
- first price
- self price
- net price
- price covering costs of production (https://iate.europa.eu/search/standard/result/1617921651988/1)
