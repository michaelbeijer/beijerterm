# Ne varietur

Legal Definition of ‘**ne varietur**’
 : it must not be changed —used as an inscription on notarized documents especially that are evidence of an encumbrance (as a mortgage)

 ‘...by virtue of a power of attorney dated [ ] 2013 (which **after being signed ne varietur** shall remain attached to the present deed to be submitted together with it to the registration formalities).’
