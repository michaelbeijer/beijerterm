# contant betaalbaar

= meteen betalen

- *Contant betaalbaar** in Belgian legal and business usage typically means *payable without delay* or *immediately*, rather than literally *in cash*.

If you want to be slightly more formal, you could also say something like:

 Voorschotfacturen zijn contant betaalbaar. =
 
 Advance invoices are payable upon receipt.
 Advance invoices must be paid upfront.
