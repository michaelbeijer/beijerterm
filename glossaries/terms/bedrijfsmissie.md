# bedrijfsmissie

1. # Definition
The Dutch term **bedrijfsmissie** can have different meanings depending on context. It is used both in diplomacy and trade to refer to a business delegation, and in management and corporate governance to mean a company’s mission statement.

1. # Possible meanings
- **Business mission** – A trade or business delegation, often accompanying a minister or organised by a chamber of commerce, to promote economic ties abroad.  
: Example: *Een bedrijfsmissie naar de USSR* → *A business mission to the USSR*.  
: See also: [Trade mission (Wikipedia)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trade_mission).  

- **Corporate mission / mission statement** – In business administration, *bedrijfsmissie* refers to the purpose or guiding principles of a company, often set out in a mission statement.  
: Example: *De bedrijfsmissie van Philips benadrukt innovatie en duurzaamheid.* → *Philips’ corporate mission emphasises innovation and sustainability.*  
: See also: [Mission statement (Wikipedia)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mission_statement).  

1. # Translation
Translate according to context:  
- In diplomatic and trade contexts: **business mission** (not *corporate mission*).  
- In management or company-profile contexts: **corporate mission** or **mission statement**.
