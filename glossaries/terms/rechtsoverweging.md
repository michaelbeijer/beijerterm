# rechtsoverweging

1. # Dutch
- rechtsoverweging (r.o., rov., r.ov.)

1. # English
- a numbered paragraph of legal reasoning (e.g.: “*rov. 4.16.3*”)
- legal ground
- ground for the decision, ground for a decision
- ground/reasoning
- consideration/paragraph

1. # Background
- **“rov.”** stands for **“overweging”** (short for *rechtsoverweging*), which can be translated into English as **“ground”**, **“reasoning”**, or more idiomatically, **“consideration”** or **“holding”**. It refers to a numbered paragraph or section in which the court sets out its legal reasoning or motivation for part of the decision.
So, for example:<blockquote>*zie rov. 4.11*

means

- see consideration 4.11*

or

- see paragraph 4.11 of the court’s reasoning*</blockquote>
