# holglas

1. # Dutch
- holglas

1. # English
- hollow glass
- container glass
- hollow glassware 
- container glassware 
 In industrial contexts, holglas refers to hollow glass products such as bottles, jars, flasks, and other containers, as opposed to [(flat glass)](vlakglas)(vlakglas), which is used for windows, façades, and mirrors.

1. # Usage examples
- "Refractaire en afdichtingsmaterialen voor vlak- en holglasproductie met toepassingen in smelten, vormen en koelen."  
→ Refractory and sealing materials for flat and hollow glass production with applications in melting, forming, and cooling. (Example phrase from a Dutch technical brochure on refractory and sealing materials.)

- "België kent een sterke traditie in de productie van holglas, waaronder bierflessen en voedingsverpakkingen."  
→ Belgium has a strong tradition in the production of hollow glass, including beer bottles and food packaging. (FEVE – European Container Glass Federation, 'Container Glass', https://feve.org/container-glass/)
