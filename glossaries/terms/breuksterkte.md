# breuksterkte

See also: breuksterkte van de vloer = floor strength limitation

1. # Dutch
- breuksterkte
- breukvastheid

1. # English
- breaking strength (maximum stress which a material is capable of developing in tension, compression, shear)
- fracture strength
- fracture performance
- ultimate strength
- break resistance
- breakage resistance
- resistance to fracture
- (als gevolg van vermoeiing) fatigue strength
- (Textile)  breaking stress (**Definition:** The maximum stress developed in a specimen stretched to rupture.The force is usually related to the area of the unstrained specimen.If the actual stress,defined in terms of the area of the strained specimen,is used,then its maximum value is called the actual breaking stress. **Definition reference:** THE TEXTILE INSTITUTE,MANCHESTER,1975 (https://iate.europa.eu/search/result/1739218363994/1))
