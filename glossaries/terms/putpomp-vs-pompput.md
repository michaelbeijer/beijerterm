1. # ‘Sump pump’ vs ‘pump sump’

| Term | Meaning | Function | Common in | Example usage |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **sump pump** | The **device** (a pump) | A pump used to remove water that has collected in a sump | Everyday English, US building codes, hardware stores | *The sump pump failed during the storm.* |
| **pump sump** | The **structure** (a pit) | A sump (pit) designed to **house a pump** and collect liquid for pumping | Engineering, wastewater treatment, patents | *The wastewater is collected in a pump sump before being processed.* |

1. # ‘Inverted drainage well’ vs ‘pump sump’
While both systems are designed to manage water, an **inverted drainage well** relies on gravity to disperse water into the ground, making it passive, whereas a **pump sump** actively removes water through mechanical means.

| Term | Function | Typical Use Case |
|---|---|---|
| **Inverted drainage well** | Facilitates gravity-driven infiltration of stormwater into the ground. | Stormwater management, especially in urban settings. |
| **Pump sump** | Collects water or wastewater for removal via mechanical pumping. | Wastewater treatment plants, basements, low-lying areas. |
