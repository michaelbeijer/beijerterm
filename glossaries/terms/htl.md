# HTL

HTL / htl / h.t.l.

- **Hogere Technische School** – former Dutch and Flemish designation for higher technical education.  
- **Höhere Technische Lehranstalt** – Austrian designation for Higher Technical College.  
- **Hoger Technisch Instituut** – alternative name for technical colleges.  
- **huur tot levering** – notarial abbreviation meaning “lease until transfer”.  
- **handels- en transportlogistiek** – trade and transport logistics (sector or study programme).  
- **Heat Transfer Limit** – term in thermodynamics and engineering.  
- **High Tension Line** – term in electrical engineering.  
- **High Temperature Lubricant** – used in chemistry and engineering.  
- **Hydrothermal Liquefaction** – technology for producing biofuels.  
- **Horizontal Transmission Line** – telecom or electrical engineering.  
- **High Technology Laboratory** – research context.  
- **High Temperature Limit** – safety setting in electronics.  
- **Hierarchical Task List** – project management and IT.
