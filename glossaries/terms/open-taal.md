# open taal

See also: codebericht
1. # open taal

- **open taal** – diplomatic/telegraphic jargon for text sent in normal readable language, i.e. not in code or cipher. Equivalent English terms include: **plain language**, **plain text**, **clear text**, **uncoded text**, **unencrypted text**, or **in the clear** (telegraphic jargon).  

1. ## Dutch synonyms / variants
- **open taal**
- **in klare taal** (rare, more general Dutch usage)
- **ongecodeerde tekst**
- **niet-versleutelde tekst**

1. ## English synonyms / equivalents
- **plain language**  
- **plain text**  
- **clear text**  
- **uncoded text**  
- **unencrypted text**  
- **in the clear** (telegraphic/diplomatic usage)  
- **unciphered text**  

1. ## Notes
Used in Ministry of Foreign Affairs code telegram instructions (e.g. *“Op een codeber. mag telegr. niet in open taal worden geantwoord …”*). The point was to prohibit answering a coded message with an uncoded reply unless very specific conditions were met.
