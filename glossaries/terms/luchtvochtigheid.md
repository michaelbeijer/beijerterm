# luchtvochtigheid

The Dutch term '<nowiki/>**luchtvochtigheid**<nowiki/>' is typically understood to mean **'relative humidity**<nowiki/>' in applied technical contexts unless specifically qualified as 'absolute'. See: https://nl.wikipedia.org/wiki/Luchtvochtigheid

- **“Luchtvochtigheid” is a general term**, encompassing both relative and absolute forms.
- **In nearly all practical contexts** (including agriculture, printing, building materials, HVAC, fungal growth, etc.), what is meant is:   → **relative humidity** (relatieve luchtvochtigheid)

- **Absolute humidity** (absolute luchtvochtigheid) is more niche — used mainly in diffusion modeling or lab-grade atmospheric calculations.
