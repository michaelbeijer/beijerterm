# toeleverancier

See also: first-tier supplier vs second-tier supplier

1. # Dutch
- toeleverancier

1. # English
- (onderleverancier) secondary supplier (IATE (https://iate.europa.eu/search/result/1746117715238/1))
- second-tier supplier (“a company that supplies materials or parts to another company that then supplies them to a manufacturer”; cf. first-tier supplier (https://dictionary.cambridge.org/dictionary/english/second-tier-supplier))
