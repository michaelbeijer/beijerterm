# proefstuk

1. # Dutch
- proefstuk

1. # English
- (generic sample) sample; test piece; test specimen; test unit 
- (proefstaaf) test bar 
- (common in metallurgy or welding) sampling coupon; test coupon (more typically used in corrosion testing or when small cut samples are taken from larger pieces)
- (coatings, materials science, and surface treatment industries) test panel (Covers the typical substrates used for standardised tests (e.g. adhesion, corrosion resistance, electrostatic charge). Matches industry norms in standards like ASTM, ISO, and EN.)
