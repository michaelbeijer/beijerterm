# voor deze

1. # Dutch
- **voor deze** = formal closing formula in official documents

1. # English equivalents
- on whose behalf  
- on behalf of  

1. # Usage example
: ''DE DIRECTEUR-GENERAAL VAN DE BUITENLANDSE ECONOMISCHE BETREKKINGEN  
voor deze:  
DE DIRECTEUR BILATERALE ZAKEN''  

: ''THE DIRECTOR-GENERAL FOR FOREIGN ECONOMIC RELATIONS  
on whose behalf:  
THE DIRECTOR OF BILATERAL AFFAIRS''  

1. # Notes
- *Voor deze* is a fixed formula in Dutch legal and diplomatic correspondence.  
- It indicates that the signatory is acting in substitution for, or by delegation of, a higher-ranking official whose name or office is given first.  
- In English, the most precise rendering is *on whose behalf*, though *on behalf of* is also acceptable.  
- This differs from simply writing *for:*, which is less formal and does not fully capture the legal nuance.
