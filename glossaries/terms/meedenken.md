# meedenken

- Meedenken* is one of those Dutch words that rarely has a neat one-to-one in English, so it’s good to have a palette of options depending on register.

Alternative versions: *dachten mee, dachten dus mee, dachtte mee, meedenker, denken mee, denken we technisch mee, denken we mee, meegedacht, dachten echt kritisch mee, dachten kritisch mee, dacht kritisch mee, kritisch meegedacht*

1. ## More idiomatic / cooperative

- *brainstorm with you*

1. ## Marketing-friendly / value-adding

1. ## Stronger / proactive nuance (when combined with *proactief*)

- *actively engage in your thinking process*
- *anticipate your needs*
- *help steer the concept*
- *partner with you in developing solutions*
