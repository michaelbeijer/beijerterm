# inhaalrustdag

1. # Dutch
- inhaalrustdag, inhaalrustdagen, inhaalrust, 

1. # English
- time off in lieu (or ‘inhaalrustdagen’: paid time off which may be taken by an employee to compensate for unpaid overtime)
- lieu days (IATE: Paid time off which may be taken by an employee to compensate for unpaid overtime.)
- time off in lieu (IATE)
- compensatory rest day
- compensation day (JurLex)

1. # IATE
 inhaalrust =
 - time off in lieu
 - lieu days
  
 Definition: a period of rest prescribed by law to compensate for the performance of overtime,which is granted in addition to the payment of an overtime premium
 Definition reference: EEIR Glossary-France,1993,p185
