1. # English
- (uitbolling) bulge 
- (van vloeistoffen) thickening, bulking
- bead, keder bead, bead keder edging (trade term)(edge with a rope/cord folded into the hem, meant to slide/lock in a profile)
- thickening, thickened portion, thickened section (Generic/functional wording)
- enlarged portion, localised thickening (Local, non‑continuous enlargement meant to bear against/engage something)
- bulge/swelling (informal)
- boss (**IATE Definition:** thickened salient part, designed to give additional strength for the insertion or attachment of another part such as a bush / **Definition reference:** José Velasco,Madrid,1991 (https://iate.europa.eu/entry/result/1561903/la-mul-nl-en)) (machined/rigid part, not textiles)
