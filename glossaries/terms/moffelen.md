1. # stoving vs baking

- **Stoving** specifically refers to heating an object, especially in an oven, to dry paint, varnish, or other coatings. It is a common term in industrial finishing and coating processes, precisely describing a heat treatment step for materials or surfaces.
- **Baking** is a broader term for cooking with dry heat, usually in an oven. While it involves heat, it is less specific to industrial coating applications and is more commonly associated with food preparation or ceramics.
