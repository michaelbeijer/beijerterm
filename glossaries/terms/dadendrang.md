# dadendrang

1. # Dutch
- dadendrang
- * 1. drang om iets te doen, m.n. om groot­se da­den te ver­rich­ten
- * 2. ge­neigd­heid tot veel ac­ti­vi­teit

1. # English
- dynamism
- drive
- thirst for action
- urge for action
- zest for action
- competitive focus
- enterprising spirit
- push
- vitality
- sense of urgency
- (onstuimigheid) impetuosity
