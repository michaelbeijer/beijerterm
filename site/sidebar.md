## About Superlookup

Welcome to **Superlookup**, one of the world's largest collections of specialist multilingual terminology.

The data is organised using two main categories: **Terms** and **Glossaries**.

- **Terms** - Individual terms with definitions and translations
- **Glossaries** - Collections of terminology from various sources
