# ontspanning

## Dutch

**ontspanning**  
Algemene technische term die een **vermindering van spanning, druk of belasting** aanduidt. De precieze betekenis is **sterk contextafhankelijk** en varieert per vakgebied (mechanica, thermodynamica, procesengineering, waterzuivering).

### Veelvoorkomende contexten
- mechanische spanning (veren, materialen)
- druk in vloeistoffen of gassen
- thermodynamische processen
- vergrendeling / ontgrendeling
- spanningsreductie na belasting

---

## English

### Common translations (context-dependent)

- **release**
- **relief**
- **relaxation**
- **pressure release**
- **pressure reduction**
- **pressure drop**
- **depressurisation / depressurization**

### Thermodynamics & fluids

- **expansion**  
  *(primarily when the subject itself is a gas or vapour)*

- **decompression**
- **pressure let-down**

### Mechanical / materials engineering

- **stress relief**
- **strain relief**
- **relaxation**

### Process & wastewater engineering

- **depressurisation**
- **pressure release**
- **pressure drop**

Used especially where a **liquid releases dissolved gas** as a result of reduced pressure (e.g. dissolved air flotation processes).

---

## Usage notes

The correct English rendering of **ontspanning** depends on **what is relaxing or losing pressure**:

- If the **medium is a gas** → *expansion* may be appropriate  
- If the **medium is a liquid under pressure** → *depressurisation*, *pressure release* or *pressure drop* is preferred  
- If the focus is on **mechanical load** → *stress relief* or *relaxation*

In technical and patent contexts, **over-literal use of “expansion” should be avoided** unless true volumetric gas expansion is intended.

## Examples

| Dutch | English |
|------|---------|
| ontspanning van een veer | release / relaxation of a spring |
| ontspanning van druk in een leiding | pressure release in a pipeline |
| ontspanning van water onder hoge druk | depressurisation of water |
| ontspanning van stoom | expansion of steam |
| ontspanning van spanning in metaal | stress relief in metal |

## Related terms

- **drukval** → pressure drop  
- **drukverlaging** → pressure reduction  
- **ontluchten** → venting / degassing  
- **ontspannen** (v.) → to relax / to release / to depressurise
